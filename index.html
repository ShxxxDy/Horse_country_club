About the Club

Horse Country Club welcomes all horse-enthusiasts and offers a wide range of services. Our members have an opportunity to train with our coaches individually, to participate in competitions and to join other social activities. The picturesque location of our facility is magnificently landscaped fields, large trees and well-maintained flower beds.

Riding Lessons

Experience The Thrill And Joy Of Horse Riding With Our Expert-Led Riding Lessons. Whether You're A Complete Beginner Or Looking To Enhance Your Skills, Our Lessons Cater To Riders Of All Levels.
